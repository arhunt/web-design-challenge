Can a location's latitude predict its weather?

For over 500 locations chosen at random, the day's maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness and windspeed were collected from the OpenWeatherMap API and loaded into a Pandas DataFrame, then each weather characteristic was plotted against the city's latitude using Matplotlib.

The resulting plots were then used to establish whether there is a correlation between the weather conditions at a particular location, and that location's distance from the equator.

Further plots, isolating the data for the Northern and Southern hemispheres, allowed for an addition of linear regressions to further determine whether a correlation existed.
